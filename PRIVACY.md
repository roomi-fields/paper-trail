# Privacy Policy — paper-trail

_Last updated: 2026-06-28_

- **Data collected by the plugin author:** none. The plugin contains no telemetry
  or analytics and sends no data to the author.
- **Third-party network access:** to acquire and verify citations, the plugin sends
  bibliographic queries (titles, authors, DOIs, search terms) to public scholarly
  services — including Crossref, arXiv, OpenAlex, Unpaywall, HAL, CORE, and the
  Internet Archive — and may use web search. Each service processes those requests
  under its own privacy policy. Downloaded PDFs and generated documents are written
  to your local machine (for example, your Obsidian vault).
- **Optional extended sources:** additional acquisition sources are disabled by
  default and require explicit opt-in (`RESEARCH_ENABLE_SHADOW_LIBS=1`). See
  `DISCLAIMER.md` and `docs/LEGAL.md`.

**Contact:** https://github.com/roomi-fields/paper-trail/issues
